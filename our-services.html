Natural/Scenic Beauty & Beaches

Ghana faces the Atlantic Ocean and is blessed with some of the world's most untouched beaches. Some are nicely maintained and are certainly among the most beautiful you will ever see. Most are places of work for fishermen who are happy to share the surf and sand with visitors

Traditional Ceremony

Life in Ghana is full of ceremonies that mark the important milestones in life.Some traditional ceremonies are; Naming Ceremony, Durbar of Chiefs, Festivals, Marrriage ceremony & Funerals.

History & Diaspora

The area now known as Ghana has a rich history of great kingdoms, tragic human misery and colonial oppression. Modern Ghana was the first country in Africa to achieve independence, was a leader in the Pan-Africa movement, and has blossomed into today's safe and stable democracy.
